## SOURCE TIERING SYSTEM

**Tier 1 (Highest Credibility):**
- Major industry reports (Gartner, Forrester, IDC, Parks Associates)
- Vendor official technical documentation (Netflix, AWS, Google Cloud)
- Academic papers and conference proceedings (ACM, IEEE)
- Government/regulatory filings (SEC, FCC, EU regulatory documents)
- Primary research data (surveys, benchmarking studies)

**Tier 2 (High Credibility):**
- Industry analyst blogs with cited data
- Trade publications (Streaming Media Magazine, VideoWeek, Broadcasting & Cable)
- Credible tech media (TechCrunch, The Verge, Ars Technica - when citing data)
- Vendor case studies and whitepapers
- Industry association reports (Streaming Video Alliance, IAB)

**Tier 3 (Moderate Credibility):**
- General news articles (when citing primary sources)
- Opinion pieces from industry experts
- Older data (>3 years) when newer unavailable
- Secondary aggregations without clear sourcing
