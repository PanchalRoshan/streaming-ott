### Key Insights from Source Discovery

**What I Learned:**

1. **Platform sources are goldmines** - Netflix Tech Blog, earnings calls contain hard operational data (CDN architecture, encoding costs, subscriber metrics). Always start here.

2. **"Follow the money" strategy works** - Public companies (Netflix, Disney, Amazon) MUST disclose key metrics in SEC filings. Investor Relations pages are Tier 1 sources.

3. **Vendor case studies reveal real numbers** - When AWS, Google Cloud, Akamai need to prove ROI, they share actual customer implementations with costs/performance.

4. **Paywall landscape identified early:**
   - Parks Associates (OTT Market Tracker) - subscription required
   - Ampere Analysis (SVOD Forecasts) - subscription required  
   - eMarketer (CTV Advertising) - subscription required
   - Strategy: Search for free summaries in trade publications that cite these reports

5. **Industry benchmark reports are Tier 1:**
   - Conviva State of Streaming (measures actual platform performance)
   - Sandvine Global Internet Phenomena (measures % of internet traffic by platform)
   - These are measurement-based, not survey/opinion-based

6. **Trade publications cite Tier 1 sources** - Streaming Media Magazine, VideoWeek often summarize paywalled analyst reports. Good secondary sources.

7. **Conference proceedings overlooked** - NAB Show, IBC, AWS re:Invent have platform case studies where Netflix/Disney engineers present actual architectures.
