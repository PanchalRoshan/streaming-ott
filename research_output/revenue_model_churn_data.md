### Key Trends
- **Hybrid Dominance:** 71% of new streaming subscribers choose ad-supported (hybrid) plans.
- **FAST Surge:** U.S. viewers streamed 1.8B hours of FAST content through Aug 2025, up from 1.3B in 2024.
- **Revenue Mix:** Free AVOD services accounted for 45.3% of revenue in 2025.

### Top Reasons for Cancellation (2025)
1. **Cutting Household Expenses:** 30% (up from 26% in 2020).
2. **Finished Specific Show:** ~25% (1 in 4 subscribers).
3. **Price Hikes:** Significant driver for SVOD churn in 2025.

### Retention Insights
- **Bundling Impact:** HBO Max-Disney+ bundle showed 59% retention after 12 months.
- **vMVPD Stability:** vMVPD services show lower churn than standalone SVOD due to "cable-like" utility.
