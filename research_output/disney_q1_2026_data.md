## Key Highlights
- **Streaming Profitability:** Entertainment SVOD operating income rose 72% to $450M.
- **Revenue Growth:** Driven by higher subscription fees (effective rates and subscriber growth) and the Fubo transaction.
- **Subscriber Reporting:** Disney has stopped reporting specific subscriber counts for Disney+ and Hulu as of Q1 FY26, focusing on financial metrics.
- **Acquisition/JV:** Formed a joint venture with Reliance Industries (Star India) on Nov 14, 2024. Disney holds a 37% interest.
- **Fubo Transaction:** Combined certain Hulu Live TV assets with FuboTV Inc. on Oct 29, 2025. Disney has a 70% interest in the combined operations.
- **Guidance:** Targeting FY26 SVOD operating margin of 10%.
