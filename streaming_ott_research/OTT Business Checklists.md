# **OTT BUSINESS CHECKLISTS**

**Strategic guides for building and scaling world-class streaming businesses.**

## **For Existing OTT Owners**

**Goal:** Verify if your platform is "**Industry-Ready**" or just "**Functional**."

- [ ] **Dynamic Device Management:** Can you remotely log out specific devices or limit concurrent streams per profile (not just per account)?  
- [ ] **Zero-Buffer Logic:** Does your player use predictive pre-fetching to load content before the user even clicks 'Play'?  
- [ ] **Server-Side Ad Insertion (SSAI):** Are your ads stitched into the stream to bypass ad-blockers and prevent playback lag?  
- [ ] **Granular Parental Controls:** Can users set individual 4-digit PINs for specific profiles (Adult vs. Kids)?  
- [ ] **Offline Download Lifecycle:** Do downloads automatically expire after a set period, and can you control download quality based on subscription tier?  
- [ ] **Deep-Link Searchability:** Can a user find your content via a Google search that leads directly into the app?  
- [ ] **Multi-Gateway Redundancy:** If one payment gateway fails, does your system automatically switch to a backup to prevent lost sales?  
- [ ] **Watch-Party/Social Sync:** Do you have features that allow users to share synchronized viewing experiences?  
- [ ] **Advanced Metadata Schema:** Is your content tagged with "Micro-Genres" and "Actor Filmographies" to power a Netflix-style recommendation engine?  
- [ ] **Code Ownership:** Do you own the source code? If not, you are renting your future from a provider who can increase prices or limit your features at any time.

## **For Aspiring OTT Founders**

**Goal:** Pre-launch essentials to ensure you don't build a platform that fails on Day 1\.

- [ ] **Niche Content Strategy:** Have you identified a specific audience (e.g., Regional, Sports, Education) rather than trying to "Beat Netflix" at everything?  
- [ ] **Scalable Architecture:** Is your backend built on a framework (like Laravel) that can handle 10,000+ concurrent users without crashing?  
- [ ] **Multi-Platform Strategy:** Are you launching on Web, Android, iOS, and Android TV simultaneously? (Missing one means losing 25% of the market).  
- [ ] **Monetization Hybridization:** Have you planned for SVOD (Subscription), AVOD (Ads), and TVOD (Pay-per-view) for maximum revenue?  
- [ ] **CDN Strategy:** Have you budgeted for a Global CDN (like BunnyCDN or AWS CloudFront) to ensure low latency for users far from your server?  
- [ ] **DRM & Security:** Do you have a plan for Digital Rights Management (DRM) to prevent users from screen-recording and pirating your content?  
- [ ] **App Store Compliance:** Does your app meet the strict "In-App Purchase" guidelines for Apple and Google to avoid rejection?  
- [ ] **User Onboarding UX:** Is your registration process under 30 seconds? (e.g., OTP or Social Login).  
- [ ] **Content Acquisition Rights:** Do you have the legal "Right to Broadcast" for every piece of content on your platform?  
- [ ] **Customization Roadmap:** Do you have a developer-ready codebase (like Streamit) that allows you to add unique features as your business grows?  
- [ ] **Expert Tip:** Most platforms fail not because of content, but because of friction. Remove the friction, and the audience will stay.
