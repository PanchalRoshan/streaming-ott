**Non-technical explanation:**  
You upload one video file. Transcoding converts it into ‚Äúdevice-ready‚Äù formats so it plays smoothly on iPhones, Android TVs, web browsers, smart TVs, etc.

**Real-life example:**  
Same movie plays on:

* iPhone on 4G (low bitrate)  
* Smart TV on Wi-Fi (high bitrate)  
* Laptop browser (DASH)  
  That only works because the video exists in multiple versions.

**Pricing anchor:**

| What drives cost | Simple estimate | Notes |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Minutes transcoded per month | New content minutes √ó renditions | More renditions \= higher cost |
